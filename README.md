### 

### Nassau Candy Distributor | Data Analytics Project

## 

## 📌 Project Overview

Nassau Candy Distributor currently relies on static factory-product allocation methods, leading to operational inefficiencies, higher lead times, and reduced profitability.

This project analyzes sales, profit, shipping performance, and factory efficiency to identify optimization opportunities and generate actionable recommendations for factory allocation decisions.

The solution combines Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI to transform raw operational data into business intelligence and decision-support insights.

\---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze factory-level operational performance
* Evaluate product profitability and efficiency
* Identify underperforming factories and products
* Build a recommendation framework for factory allocation decisions
* Improve lead time efficiency without sacrificing profitability

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Lot's O' Nuts generated the highest sales and profit.
* Wicked Choccy's showed strong operational performance.
* The Other Factory achieved the lowest average lead time.
* Sugar Shack demonstrated the lowest efficiency score.
* Wonka Bar products dominated overall sales performance.

\---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

1. Retain high-performing chocolate products in their current factories.
2. Review low-profit products for potential reassignment.
3. Improve efficiency in underperforming factories.
4. Monitor lead-time trends continuously.
5. Prioritize high-margin products during inventory planning.
